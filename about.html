Who We Are

The State Capacity Project is an initiative focused on the mechanics of governance. We explore how design, technology, and institutional practice shape the ability of states to deliver services reliably and equitably.

What We Do

Through convenings, publications, and partnerships, SCP highlights strategies and practices that strengthen institutional capacity. We work across domains and geographies to share insights and create practical tools for practitioners, policymakers, and designers.
